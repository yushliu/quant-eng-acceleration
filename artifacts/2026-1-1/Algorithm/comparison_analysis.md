# Stage 2 Preliminary Comparison Note

This note records the preliminary observable differences from the baseline portfolio first run without making a final recommendation.

- Historical Simulation produced the highest breach count in this run.
- Parametric Normal was close to Historical Simulation on average VaR/CVaR but slightly lower on breach count and the lowest on the CVaR stability proxy.
- EWMA + Monte Carlo produced the highest average VaR/CVaR and the lowest breach rate in the baseline run.

Final interpretation, tradeoff discussion, and benchmark recommendation are deferred to Stage 3.
